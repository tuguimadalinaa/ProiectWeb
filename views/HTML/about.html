About our App

We bring a new way for you to upload your files on different cloud storage tools

  • Our app provides 3 different cloud drives (GoogleDrive, DropBox and OneDrive) for managing all your files

Login:

Login is a simple page that allows you to go to our Home Page. If you are not logged you can't access any of our funcionalities

After login, a cookie will be provided to you. as long as the cookie is valid, you will be logged to our page.

Sign Up:

Sign Up allows you to have a basic account on our API. After you signed up, you have to login with that account to have access to our services.

Home Page:

We provide 3 drivers. To access all your files and have rights to modify content, you should first login.

Using Oauth Services you will now have access to all your files on each drive and you could also modify them.

To Login, you can siply just click on one of the icons in home page and you will be redirected to their login page.

After this step, you will be redirected on our home page again

About Page:

This page provides a written tutorial about how to use our API

File Storage Pages:

We have 3 pages: DropBox | OneDrive | GoogleDrive

This Pages are ment to help you see all your files.

Down on this page exists a scrollable bar that offers many operations on files.

To use one of those that implies a file already existing, just simply click once on the file and then on the button with the operation you want to do.

To navigate between folders you can just double click on one icon with folder.

Log out:

To go out of our API just click LogOut and you will redirected on login page
